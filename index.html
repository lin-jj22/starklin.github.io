About Me

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at the Intelligent Manufacturing & Precision Machining Lab, Department of Mechanical Engineering, Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Jianfu Zhang.

I received my Bachelor's degree from the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Tsinghua University, with a solid background in Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science.

My research interests focus on Augmented Reality (AR) for mechanical assembly and AI-assisted assembly instruction authoring and generation.
